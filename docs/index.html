Teaching and Learning Materials:

Textbooks:

(in the order of 1st edition publication year)
  • Doing Bayesian Data Analysis: A Tutorial with R, JAGS, and Stan
    • by John Kruschke; 1st edition in 2012, 2nd edition in 2014
  • Bayesian Essentials with R
    • by Jean-Michel Marin and Christian P. Robert; 1st edition in 2014; R package bayess
  • Statistical Rethinking - A Bayesian Course with Examples in R and Stan
    • by Richard McElreath; 1st edition in 2016, 2nd edition in 2020; R package rethinking
  • Bayesian Statistical Methods
    • by Brian J. Reich and Sujit K. Ghosh; 1st edition in 2019
  • Probability and Bayesian Modeling
    • by Jim Albert and Monika Hu; 1st edition in 2019; R package ProbBayes

Course materials:
